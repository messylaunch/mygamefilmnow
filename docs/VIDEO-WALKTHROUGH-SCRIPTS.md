# Walkthrough Video Scripts
### Exactly which screens to record and what to say

Nine short videos (30–90 sec each — short beats long; people rewatch short). Record with a guided-demo tool (Arcade / Storylane / Supademo) or plain screen recording. Scripts are word-for-word; screens are in order.

---

## FAN SERIES (host on start. + link in receipt texts)

### V1 — "How to watch a game" (60s) — THE most important video
Screens: app. home → search "Eagles" → team page → Friday's game → Buy Stream → checkout → video unlocked → My Games.
> "Here's how to watch any game on GameFilmNow, start to finish. Go to app.mygamefilmnow.com and type your team's name — or your town, or even your player's name. There's the team. Every game they play is right here — season games AND tournament games, together. Tap Friday's game. You can buy the live stream, the recording after the game, or the bundle for the best deal. Tap Buy, put in your card, and — that's it. The video unlocks right here on the same page. No emails to dig through, no links to hunt for. It's also saved forever under My Games, and we text you the link just in case. Three clicks, and you never miss a game again."

### V2 — "Live stream vs recording vs passes" (45s)
Screens: game page products panel → team pass → tournament page passes.
> "Quick guide to the tickets. The live stream is for game night — watch it as it happens; it comes off sale automatically once the game ends. The recording is the full edited game — every play, multiple cameras — ready within 72 hours, yours forever. The bundle gets you both, cheaper. Following the whole season? The Team Pass covers every game. And during tournaments, one Tournament Pass covers your team's entire run."

### V3 — "Follow your family" (45s)
Screens: player profile → Follow → My Feed with "because you follow" → family list add.
> "Got a player you love? Follow them. Now every game they play shows up in your feed — see the tag? 'Because you follow Zach Riddle.' You'll get reminders before every game so you never find out on Monday that you missed Saturday. And check this — search any relative on GameFilmNow, add them to your family list, and they'll see you cheering from their side too."

## TEAM SERIES (send to coaches during onboarding)

### V4 — "Your coach dashboard in 3 minutes" (90s)
Screens: coach.html stats → schedule grid inline edit → stream window → team page editor → sponsors → revenue.
> "Coach, this is your whole season on one screen. Every game is one row — like a spreadsheet, because it basically is one. Change a kickoff time? Click, type, done — it's live. Stream prices, recording prices, your call, per game. See these stream windows? Set the start and end once, and the stream puts itself on sale and takes itself down. Nobody has to remember anything on game night. Over here is your public page: your bio, your roster, your merch — and your sponsor slots, which are YOUR ad space; you keep every dollar of sponsor money. And this number at the top? That's what your families raised this season just by watching your games."

### V5 — "Inviting your players" (60s)
Screens: roster tab → invite players → parent consent explainer → pass status.
> "Getting your kids into the film room takes one screen. Enter each player's name and their PARENT'S email and phone — we never contact kids directly. Every parent gets the Player Pass invite and a consent form. The moment they pay and sign, both accounts activate — one for the parent, one for the player. Your dashboard shows exactly who's in and who needs a nudge, and the nudge goes to the parents with one tap."

### V6 — "Running film like the pros" (90s)
Screens: team. coach view → post question on Play 14 → homework tracker → calendar event.
> "This is where your team gets better. Film's back within 24 hours of every game, cut play by play. Pick any play and pin a question — 'What hole should we hit here? Everyone answers by Friday.' Every player gets notified, and this tracker shows who's done their homework and who's ducking you. Add your film session to the team calendar and parents get the reminder automatically. Thirty minutes a week — there's a whole playbook we give you called the 30-Minute Film Session — and your Tuesdays will never be the same."

## PLAYER SERIES (for the kids — keep the energy up)

### V7 — "Your film room" (60s)
Screens: team. dashboard → film room → next play → like → My List → comment with @.
> "This is YOUR film room. Every game you play shows up here within a day, play by play — tap through every snap like the pros do. See something clean? Hit the heart. Want to study it later? Add it to your list. Got a question? Ask coach right on the play — and tag your teammates with the @ sign, like 'at-DeShawn nice block.' They get notified. Everything coach posts lands in your notifications, so check the bell."

### V8 — "Tag yourself, build your tape" (75s)
Screens: tag me button → my plays list → highlight unlock → clip picker → music → profile montage.
> "Here's the feature that follows you your whole career. See a play you made? Hit 'Tag me.' That play goes on YOUR reel — and it stays with you after the season, year after year, team to team. When you've got a season's worth, Highlight Mode unlocks everything: download every one of your plays — no screen recording, ever — then pick your best twenty, choose your soundtrack, and we build your highlight tape automatically. It goes right on your public profile where your family — and yeah, scouts — can watch it."

## ADMIN SERIES (internal — for you and staff)

### V9 — "The schedule grid" (90s)
Screens: admin stats → grid inline edits → add row → CSV import → row modal → teams tab → orders/refund → staff roles.
> "One screen runs GameFilmNow. Every game is a row: team, event, date, time, opponent, stream window, prices, score, crew. Edit anything inline. New game? Add Row. Whole season? Import the CSV straight from a spreadsheet. The pencil opens the full editor — links, photos, bracket uploads. Teams tab: a team with three divisions is one team, three squads, sixty seconds — not fifteen WordPress posts. Orders tab: refund one purchase, or refund an entire cancelled game in one batch — buyers get notified automatically. Staff tab: editors, recorders, uploaders, coaches, scouts — one person can wear two hats."

**Recording tips:** use the templates with demo data (that's what they're for) · 1080p, hide bookmarks bar · phone-record V1 and V7 vertically too, for social · re-record V1 whenever checkout changes — it's the support-ticket killer.
