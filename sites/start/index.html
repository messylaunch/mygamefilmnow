Questions

Everything families ask us.

How do I watch a game I bought?

You watch it on the exact page where you bought it — the video unlocks the moment payment goes through. It's also saved forever under My Games in your account, and we text you the link so you can't lose it.

What happens if I buy a live stream and the game already ended?

You can't — streams automatically come off sale when the game ends. The page forwards you to the full recording, which is ready within 72 hours.

What's the difference between a Team Pass and a Tournament Pass?

A Team Pass covers every game your team plays in its regular season. A Tournament Pass covers every game a specific team plays in one tournament. You can buy either right from the team's page.

Is this safe for my kid?

Yes — that's the whole design. Player accounts require a signed parent consent form, all texts and emails go to the parent (never the child), there's no open chat, and the film room is a private team study space, not social media.

What does the $75 Player Pass include?

Full film-room access for one player and their parent: every game play-by-play within 24 hours, comments and coach questions, self-tagging, a public player profile, and a Top-20 family list that gets automatic game reminders.

Do you sell my kid's information?

Never. Scout accounts can view film and profiles, but any contact request routes to the parent. We never share a child's contact info — we never even collect it.

Our team films our own games. Can we still use GameFilmNow?

Yes — that's Camera School. Get certified with your equipment, pay a one-time season fee, and sell your games on our platform with the full toolkit.
